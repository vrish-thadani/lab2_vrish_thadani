Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, known as the "Missile Man of India," was a scientist, engineer, and the 11th President of India. Born in 1931 in Rameswaram, he rose from humble beginnings to lead India’s space and missile programs and inspire millions through his words and life. He dedicated his life to education, youth empowerment, and national development.

Why I Admire Him

  • He contributed to India’s nuclear and missile programs.
  • He remained humble and connected to students and the public.
  • He promoted science, education, and dreaming big despite adversity.
